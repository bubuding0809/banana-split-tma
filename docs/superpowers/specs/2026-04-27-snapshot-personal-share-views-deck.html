Design spec · 2026-04-27

Snapshot detail
Personal-share views

Reframe the snapshot detail page so its breakdowns answer the question the page is already asking — "how much did you spend?". Drop the "By Payer" tab in both the mini app and the bot's group message.

Status: Awaiting user review Author: bubu (with Claude Opus 4.7) Schema changes: 0 New tRPC: 0 Surfaces: TMA + bot
Why this exists

The numbers don't add up to the user

The hero card says "You spent SGD 174.16". The bars and group totals immediately below sum to SGD 1,886.97 — the trip's total spend. Each row also shows the full expense amount, not the user's share.

It's not wrong data, it's the wrong question. The page is "your view of this snapshot." The breakdowns should answer "where did your $174 go?" — by category, by date.

Today (mismatch)

  • Hero anchor: $174.16 (your share)
  • Category bars: sum to $1,886.97
  • Row amount: full expense (e.g. $323.27)
  • Three tabs: Category / Date / Payer

After this spec

  • Hero anchor: $174.16 (unchanged)
  • Category bars: sum to $174.16
  • Row amount: your share (e.g. $32.30, red)
  • Two tabs: Category / Date
Decision · brainstorm output

Why "By Payer" is removed

Three reasonable meanings, none of them clean enough to keep:

OptionBars sum toVerdict
A. "Where my share came from" — group my share by payer $174.16 consistent but the value-add over By Category + the Shares section is small
B. "Who paid the most cash" (current behavior) $1,886.97 inconsistent with the "You spent" anchor
C. Net to/from each payer (settlement-flavored) — overlaps with existing settlement features; mixed-sign bars are ugly

Decision: drop the tab entirely. Cleaner page, fewer toggles, no semantic confusion. Same call applies to the bot's group message — the Payer button goes away there too.

Edge cases · resolved

Five details, five calls

Group count

Your count only. "26 Apr · 9 your share" — count of expenses you have a share in, not the day's total expense count.

Zero-share rows

Drop entirely. If your share is $0, the row doesn't appear in the grouped lists. The full expense list (main page) is unaffected.

Per-row layout

Mirror ChatExpenseCell. Right column: date / red share amount / "share" caption. Full expense amount no longer shown in row.

Empty groups

Disappear. Categories or dates where you have no share at all aren't rendered — no bar, no list section.

Section label

Add the qualifier. BY CATEGORY → BY CATEGORY · YOUR SHARE. Same for date. Disambiguates from the group-totals story without needing a tooltip.

Surface 2 · bot group message

Bot message — drop the Payer button

The bot message lives in the group chat. There's no single "you" to anchor on, so totals stay. Only change: remove the Payer toggle button + its render branch. The "🧾 Shares" section already shows per-person amounts and stays.
